Who is #theHUFF?

First off, I would like to thank you for coming to my page. It's not much but it's a start. It's a wonderful journey so far too.


My name is David Huffman and I'm a coder... programmer... student of sorts. There we go, that's good verbiage. I am a student and let me tell you about my journey.


I've always had an interest in the digital world with a heavy emphasis on digital and content marketing. I owned a few websites, had a blog, podcast, etc... I was never happy though. Stuff was never developed how I wanted and I had more ideas I wanted to play with than I had people to implement those ideas.


Fast forward to November of 2021 and I remembered that one time I tried to learn Spanish on my phone. It was on an app and I thought what else is on an app. I stumpled accross Grasshopper and downloaded it. I ate it up. Aboslutely loved it.


Then a disc golf buddy, mentor and owner of a software company had to burst my bubble, "You think Javascript is fun? Just wait until you learn html and css too"... which lead me to Mimo and Udemy and a lot of other sources of information. Information overload and no clear road map to my destination.


Then as fate would have it I was working on Facebook one day designing a sales funnel and BOOM! "Coding Bootcamp" popped up. I immediately asked my buddy and did some research and figured I couldn't go wrong. It would be a great oppurtunity to level up as a digital marketer and entrepreneur and give me the ability to change my career if ever need be.


And that brings us to now. You're reading this because of the events that unfolded. It's not much and it's not perfect but it's mine and I urge you to take a look below at my portfolio. Thank you for stopping by!
